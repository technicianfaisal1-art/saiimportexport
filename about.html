Our Story

Bridging the gap between Indian farmers and global markets with uncompromising quality and trust.

Who We Are

Established with a vision to take India's finest agricultural produce to the world, SAI Import Export Agro has grown into a trusted name in the global export market. We specialize in sourcing, processing, and exporting premium rice directly from the heartlands of India.

Our deep-rooted relationships with local farming communities allow us to ensure fair trade practices while maintaining strict quality control from farm to port. By bypassing traditional middlemen, we empower farmers while delivering cost-effective, premium-grade products to our global B2B partners.

Whether it is the long grains of 1121 Basmati, the everyday utility of Sona Masoori, or the bulk processing of IR 64 Parboiled rice, we deliver nothing but the best. Our commitment to sustainability, fair trade, and uncompromised quality has made us the go-to sourcing partner for rice buyers across the globe.
