Our Certifications

Internationally
Recognized Standards

We don't just claim quality — we prove it. Every certification we hold is renewed, audited, and actively maintained.

APEDA

APEDA Registered

Agricultural & Processed Food Products Export Development Authority

APEDA is India's government body that regulates and promotes exports of agricultural products. Registration is mandatory for all rice exporters. Our APEDA number is verified and active.

Your shipment is legally authorized for export from India under government regulation.

FSSAI

FSSAI Certified

Food Safety and Standards Authority of India

FSSAI is India's apex food regulatory body. Our FSSAI license confirms that our processing facilities meet India's food safety standards — covering hygiene, storage, and handling.

The rice you import has been processed in a hygiene-audited, food-safe facility.

ISO
22000

ISO 22000 Certified

International Organization for Standardization — Food Safety Management

ISO 22000 is a globally accepted food safety management system standard. It covers the entire supply chain — from raw material sourcing to final shipment — ensuring zero contamination risk.

Our entire supply chain — farm to port — meets international food safety management standards.

HACCP

HACCP Compliant

Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points

HACCP is a systematic preventive approach to food safety. It identifies, evaluates, and controls biological, chemical, and physical hazards in food production — accepted by USA, EU, and GCC importers.

Hazards are identified and eliminated before your cargo leaves our facility.

Our Process

How We Ensure
Zero Compromise

A strict 6-step quality control process — from farm selection to vessel loading.

1

Farm Sourcing

Directly from verified, certified farming communities in Punjab, Haryana & Andhra Pradesh.

2

Raw Inspection

Grain samples tested for moisture, purity, and contamination before procurement.

3

Processing

Milling, sorting, and grading in our FSSAI-certified, ISO-compliant processing units.

4

Sortex Cleaning

Optical sortex machines remove broken grains, discolorations, and foreign matter.

5

Pre-Shipment Test

Third-party SGS/BV lab testing on final packed cargo before container sealing.

6

Vessel Loading

Supervised container stuffing with moisture-proof lining at major Indian ports.
